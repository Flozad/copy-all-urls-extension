Umbrella is ready

Copy every open tab to your clipboard, and paste a list of links to reopen them. Here are the three things worth knowing.

Getting started

  1. Copy all your tabs

    Click the Umbrella icon in your toolbar, or press Ctrl+Shift+U. Every open tab lands on your clipboard.

  2. Paste a list to reopen it

    Press Ctrl+Shift+Y, or use Paste in the popup, and Umbrella opens each link in a new tab. Right-clicking any page gives you both actions too.

  3. Pick your format

    URL only, text, HTML, JSON, delimited, or a custom template using $url, $title, and $date. Set a default in Settings.

Your data

Umbrella works entirely on your device. It requests no host permissions, loads nothing from the network, and never transmits your URLs or clipboard contents anywhere.
